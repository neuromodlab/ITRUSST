Summary

Til shared updates on what has happened so far regarding FUN22 conference. The local organising committee has been working on the conference website, Brainbox sponsorship, venue, dinner venues and setting up hybrid zoom. Also, the scientific committee responsible for the scientific programme has been formed, soon they will meet to discuss presentation formats, invitations and other issues. The next steps are inviting speakers, finding sponsors, the call for abstracts in early April and then the deadline for abstract submission in early June. In June, the abstracts will be reviewed and selected and feedback on abstract acceptance will be given in July. The discussion point Til raised concerned the virtual gathertown satellite poster event. A suggestion was made to instead the gathertown approach use a different format that the posters are uploaded and create an infrastructure so that remote attendees can comment and ask questions to have as many interactions during the few days itself. There was also a brainstorm on living documentation for the documents of the working groups, here possible platforms have to be explored. Lastly, there is an open call for controversial discussion topics so that these can be included as interactive parts in the conference streaming.
